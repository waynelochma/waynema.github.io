About Me

Hi! I'm Wayne and I like to make stuff! Ever since my first shop class in high school, I knew I wanted to be an engineer. As far as I can remember, I have always loved taking things apart and learning how to put them back together. From a small solar powered car that I made with my father when I was ten, to the computer where I'm writing this today, being a maker let's me express myself in a way that words can't. As I pursue a Mechanical Engineering degree at the University of Toronto, I am always looking for projects to learn and improve my technical skills. Come learn a little more about me!
